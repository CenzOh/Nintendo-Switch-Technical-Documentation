Switch Lite

Nintendo Switch Lite is an affordable option in the Switch family that is dedicated to handheld play. Here is what the Nintendo Switch Lite offers:

Play Modes:
Only available in Handheld Mode
Compatible games:
Nintendo Switch games that support Handheld Mode
Joy-Con Controllers:
Joy-Con controllers are not included. Note: System does not include HD Rumble or IR Motion Controller
Battery Life:
Approximately 3-7 Hours. Mode Number: HDH-001. The battery life depends on which software you play. For instance, playing The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild will last your battery approximately 4 hours.
